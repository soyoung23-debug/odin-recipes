The Best Banana Cake You'll Ever Have

Ever wonder what to do with these bananas?

Well, throwing them is not an option. Use this recipe instead, and be prepared...

Ingredients

You will need the following:

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1.5 pcs banana
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • walnuts for toppings, to your liking

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

Here's what you will do:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Line the loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas, then add the eggs, greek yogurt, butter and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until combined. Stir in the chocolates and nuts.
  5. Scrape the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake the bread for 50-60 minutes, until a toothpic inserted comes out clean.
  7. Enjoy!! No really, enjoy it!!
